How prediction market arb works: Each contract pays $1 if the event happens, $0 if not. If the same event is priced at 40¢ on Platform A and 60¢ on Platform B, you Buy YES on A for 40¢ and Buy NO on B for 40¢ (NO = 1 − 60¢). Total cost: 80¢. Guaranteed payout: $1. Locked-in profit: 20¢ — regardless of outcome. Fees are deducted per-platform on your winnings. Net spread = profit after fees. Negative net spread = fees eat the entire profit; skip it.
